## 1. This Is a Supplemental Tool — Not an Emergency Line

The Emergency Response Offline SDK is an auxiliary digital reporting tool designed to assist outdoor recreationists, anglers, hunters, boaters, and hikers in transmitting location-based emergency information to participating state agency operators **when cellular voice service is unavailable or degraded.**

This system does **not** connect to:
- Public Safety Answering Points (PSAPs)
- 911 Communications Centers
- Emergency Medical Services (EMS) dispatch
- Police Computer-Aided Dispatch (CAD) systems (in this pilot version)

Operators monitoring this system are **not** certified 911 dispatchers and are **not** obligated to provide real-time emergency response guarantees.

---

## 2. Call 911 First

If any of the following are true, **stop using this app and call 911 immediately:**

- You have any cellular signal (even one bar)
- You are near a landline telephone
- You are near another person who can call for help
- The emergency involves immediate threat to life
- You need an ambulance, fire department, or law enforcement response

**911 is always the primary emergency resource.** This system is intended for scenarios where 911 is genuinely unavailable due to lack of coverage in remote areas.

---

## 3. Technology Limitations

**GPS Accuracy:** Location data is derived from device GPS, which may be inaccurate by 5–200+ meters depending on device, canopy cover, terrain, and atmospheric conditions. The coordinates you transmit may not precisely reflect your location.

**GPS Cache ("Stale Location"):** If a live GPS fix cannot be obtained, this system may transmit your last known location, which may be up to 5 minutes old. A warning will display when stale data is used.

**Connectivity Dependency:** This system attempts to transmit your emergency packet over cellular data or Wi-Fi. In areas with no signal, packets are queued on your device and transmitted when connectivity is restored. There is **no guarantee** of delivery in areas without connectivity.

**Battery Dependency:** Emergency packet transmission requires a functioning device with sufficient battery. Low battery conditions may interrupt transmission.

**System Availability:** This system is provided on a best-effort basis during its pilot phase. Uptime, transmission success, and operator response time are not guaranteed.

---

## 4. Response Time Is Not Guaranteed

Operator availability varies. This system does not guarantee:
- Any minimum response time
- That an operator will be monitoring at the time of your emergency
- That resources can be dispatched to your location
- That your location is within the service coverage area

**This system is currently operating in a pilot/demonstration phase.** It has not been certified, approved, or endorsed by any public safety authority, the State of Michigan, or any federal agency. It is provided for evaluation and demonstration purposes only.

**Do not rely on this system as your sole means of emergency communication.**

## 6. Geographic Coverage

This pilot system covers demonstration resources within the State of Michigan. If your device GPS indicates a location outside Michigan, the system will display a warning and show the nearest available Michigan resource for demo purposes. This does not reflect real-world coverage outside Michigan.
